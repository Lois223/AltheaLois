DocuCare is an advanced web-based clinical documentation system designed for the Pediatrics Department. It offers comprehensive features, including charting, progress notes, care plans, doctors' orders, and nurse and room management. By streamlining workflows and enhancing documentation accuracy, DocuCare supports efficient patient care and better coordination among healthcare teams.
